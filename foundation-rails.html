About

With a common passion for motorsports and sustainability, members of the Waterloo Hybrid team come from a wide variety of academic backgrounds. Each member, with their unique talents and interests, are able to pursue these endeavours through the Hybrid project. The up-and-coming team is comprised of students from many different faculties from the University of Waterloo. The core design values of the team are to be reliable, innovative and efficient.
